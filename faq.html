Frequently asked

Everything you might want to know before your first story.

What exactly is a decant?

A decant is a small amount of a genuine fragrance, carefully transferred from the original bottle into a clean sample vial. Every Scent Stories decant is 2 ml of real, authentic juice — roughly 30–40 sprays, enough to wear a scent for a week or two and really get to know it.

Are these authentic? Not fakes or clones?

100% authentic. We buy genuine full bottles and decant from them — we never sell clones, dupes or imitations. What you smell on your skin is exactly what the house intended.

Is reselling decants even legal?

Yes. We resell authentic product and name each fragrance only to describe what's inside the vial. Scent Stories is an independent curator — we are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any brand we feature, and we don't use their logos or imagery.

What are the "surprise" scents in a set?

Each story reveals two fragrances up front; the rest are chosen by us to complete the mood. It's the fun part — you discover bottles you'd never have picked yourself, all tied to the feeling of the set.

How do mystery boxes work?

You pick the vibe — Hype, Niche, Artist's Cut or Wildcard — and we curate the contents. You don't choose the exact scents; that's the whole point. Every box is built to fit its world, with room for something well above its price to slip in.

If I love one, can I buy the full bottle?

Absolutely — that's the idea. Find your signature in 2 ml, and we'll point you to the full bottle and credit the cost of your decant toward it.

When can I actually order?

Scent Stories is a concept in the making. Join the list and you'll be first to know when the first drop goes live — plus a founding-member discount.
