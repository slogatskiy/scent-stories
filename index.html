Our story

We don't sell samples. We sell somewhere to go.

Anyone can mail you five vials. That isn't what this is.

Scent Stories is built around emotion. Every advent calendar, every mystery box, every set is made to take you somewhere. A summer in Italy. A midnight in Berlin. A first date you'll want to remember.

So we do the hunting. We obsess over niche houses, artistic perfumers and the bottles the internet won't shut up about, then curate them into small sets built around a feeling. A city. A season. A night you want to remember.

Every decant is 2 ml of real, authentic juice. You're not just trying perfume. You're collecting moments, and finding the one that becomes yours.
