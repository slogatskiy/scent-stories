Our story

Fragrance, without the gamble.

Scent Stories began with a simple frustration: discovering perfume is expensive, and a full bottle bought blind is a $200 mistake waiting to happen.

So we do the hunting. We obsess over niche houses, artistic perfumers and the bottles the internet won't shut up about — then curate them into small sets built around a feeling. A city. A season. A night you want to remember.

Every decant is 2 ml of real, authentic juice. Explore five at a time, find the one that's yours, and we'll point you to the full bottle — with your decant credited.
